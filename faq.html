FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you have any enquiry on Vanilla Card, how to claim grant and others. Proceed to read the following

Q: How do I receive Funds ?

Ans: Funds will be sent to your Vanilla Card

Q: How much do I qualify for?

Ans: Qualificatin amount is based on your available balance

Q: How much Minimum Balance does my card needs to have?

Ans: The minimum balance you require on your card is $100

Q: What is the 10% Minimum Balance ?

Ans: The 10% minimum balance is the required balance to request grants . If you request for $10000, you will need to have a minimum balance of $1000 , if $5000, a minimum of $500 is required as card balance

Q: Do I need to pay this grant back?

Ans: No, this grant doesn't have to be paid back

Q: What can the grant be used for ?

Ans: This is a personal and loyalty reward, it can be used for anything

Q: Where do I get Vanilla Card?

Ans: Pick your vanilla card at a retailer near you.

Q: Where do I get Vanilla Card?

Ans: Once the 10% has been confirmed. Payment will be made available in your prepaid card within 24-hours

Q: What happens to my 10% Minimum balance ?

Ans: This funds remain yours and can be withdrawn anytime. The funds are only used to confirm the right grant amount is sent out to our customers
