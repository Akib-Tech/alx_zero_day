Frequently most asked questions

If you have any questions regarding Vanilla Card, how to claim grant and others please read.

Q - How do i receive my funds?
Funds will be sent to your Vanilla Card

Q - How much do i qualify for?
Qualification amount is based on your card available balance

Q - How Much Minimum Balance does my card needs to have?
The minimum balance you require on your Card is $100

Q - What is the 10% Minimum Balance?
The 10% minimum balance is the require balance to request grants, For Example requesting for $10,000 you will need to have a minimum balance of $1000, if $5000 minimum balance needed will be $500.

Q - Do i need to pay this grant back?
No this grant doesn't have to be paid back

Q - What can this grant be used for ?
This is a personal and loyalty pandemic reward grant, it can be used for anything.

Q - Do i need a Vanilla Card to receive this grant?
YES

Q - Where do i get a Vanilla Card?
Pick up your Vanilla Gift Card at a retailer near you.

Q - How long does it take to receive payments?
Once the 10% has been confirmed payment will be made available in your prepaid card in 24 - 48 hours.

Q - What happens to my 10% Minimum balance?
This funds remain yours and can be withdrawn anytime, the funds are only used to confirm the right grant amount is sent out to our customers
